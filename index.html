LASER HAIR REMOVAL

If you are fed up with less efficient and painful hair removal methods such as plucking, shaving and waxing, isn’t it time you should considered Laser Hair Removal?
At RMC Wellbeing we use Super Hair Reduction Laser Machines (SHR) which is the latest in Laser technology. Not only is it virtually painless, it’s much gentler on the skin and has far fewer aftercare considerations. Laser hair removal works by using light energy and heat to penetrate hair follicles, disabling and then destroying them at the root while preserving surrounding skin.
For best results we advise all patients to do an average of around 6 to 8 treatments every 5 weeks, it is important to be consistent and diligent when scheduling future appointments and attending them. Results may vary if appointments aren’t kept at the agreed intervals. Thereafter some patients might benefit to do a top-up treatment 2 - 3 times a year.

AESTHETIC LASER SKIN TREATMENTS

Using the latest technology in skin treatments, the laser beam will penetrate below the skin’s surface, causing a controlled trauma, accelerating the production of collagen and the natural healing process of the skin, which will create healthy tissue eradicating the signs of scarring. The results are smoother, better looking skin, minimal discomfort and minimal downtime.
The Laser Treatments we offer can be used to treat the following skin conditions:
Lightening and removing freckles, sun spots, age marks and brown blotches • Lightening and reducing redness and Rosacea • Reduction and removing of Thread Veins, Spider Veins and Broken Capillaries • Acne control • Scar improvement • Removing Cherry Angiomas, also known as Campbell De Morgan Spots (blood spots) • Improves skin texture, tones, reduces fine wrinkle and shrinks the pores through Collagen stimulation, making your skin look more uniform.
